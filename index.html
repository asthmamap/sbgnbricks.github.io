Welcome to the SBGN Bricks project, an effort focused on describing recurring patterns in biological networks that correspond to such biological concepts as metabolic reaction, catalysis, inhibition, protein phosphorylation, complex formation, transcription, translation etc.

Brick highlight

Catalysis: multiple substrates and products. The enzyme catalyses an irreversible metabolic process which consumes two substrates S1 and S2 and produces two products P1 and P2.
